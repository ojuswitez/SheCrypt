Objective

SheCrypt is a Tezos India initiative aimed to increase awareness about the women in crypto, their achievements and opportunities for women. With blockchain being a booming sector that has huge potential, we are here to support budding female blockchain enthusiasts in their journey. This initiative also encourages women to learn about blockchain and be inspired by what the industry has to offer. Blockchain has already improved business operation in many sectors and ShecCypt believes women are going to drive this innovation forward!
